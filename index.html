Who I am

Web Developer in Japan

After graduating from business school with a degree in Economics & International Business and a Mathematics minor I went on a little e-commerce excursion, surviving completely on income from my eBay store. I moved to Japan in 2016 for what was supposed to be a gap year of teaching English but turned into 6+ years of living in Japan! I passed the most difficult level of the Japanese Language Profiency Test (JLPT N1), got an English teaching certification, moved up in the English education industry and did a lot of translation work. But I finally realized I needed some kind of solid skill that is in high demand. That's where web development came in.

A couple of my software engineering friends in Texas told me to try out some lessons on Free Code Camp. Two FCC certifications later and a semester of Computer Science online from my college in Texas and I'm now a freelance web developer, also open to full-time work for the right company and working environment where I can thrive.

Contact me and let's work together!
