What I do

Management

What sets me apart from many other Shopify developers is I actually manage my own stores on Shopify and various marketplaces so I have a real need to understand how to make changes to my store from the perspective of a business owner. Many Shopify developers do not have this real experience. They started learning how to do e-commerce development to get a paycheck.

Development

I build new Shopify stores for people who have a product they want to sell to the world and don’t know how to start, people who want to start dropshipping, people who are already selling on another platform or marketplace (like ebay or Wix) and want to move to a better platform where they are in more control, and even people who have a physical business but haven’t tried selling online yet.

Consulting

If you haven’t started selling to a global customer base online, let me help you! There's a whole world of REVENUE waiting for you. I can do anything from making small changes to your store, consulting, teaching you how to fix problems, various optimizations such as SEO, or even just doing everything for you so you can focus on other aspects of your business.

My work

Who I am

Web Developer in Japan

After graduating from university with a degree in Economics & International Business and Mathematics minor, I went on a little e-commerce excursion, surviving on income from my eBay store. I had been fascinated with e-commerce from an early age, starting out on ebay at 10 years old.

I moved to Japan in 2016 for what was supposed to be a gap year of teaching English but turned into 7 years of living in Japan! I passed the most difficult level of the Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT N1), got an English teaching certification, helped an English school start-up as the head teacher, and did a lot of translation work. But I finally realized I needed some kind of solid skill that is in high demand. That's where web development came in.

A couple of my software engineering friends in Texas told me to try out some lessons on Free Code Camp. Two FCC certifications later and a semester of Computer Science online from my college in Texas and I'm now a freelance web developer, also open to full-time work for the right company and working environment where I can thrive.

Contact me and let's work together!
